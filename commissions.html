Commissions

I love making personalised pieces for people and work in a range of mediums, so there’s plenty to choose from. The process is super simple, simply choose a type of medium (digital, ink, gouache), a size (range: A5, A4, A3) and a description on what you’d like made and we’ll go from there!

Digital pieces make a wonderful gift! They are a modern alternative to physical media. All pieces are made ready for print and can be reproduced multiple times in various forms, think prints, stickers, tshirts or mugs!

My ink pieces are usually characterised by two words, graphic and detailed. I love producing pieces that are packed with little fine lines and hidden tokens, contrasted against bold swarths of black. My inking pieces are all drawn with extra love and care and come in a variety of colours if you’re looking for a fun alternative asides from the usual black!

For lovers of traditional media gouache is a timeless and gorgeous choice. Every piece is carefully sketched out before layers of paint are added. I love the look of bold blocks of colour and love to mix up the texture of the paintings with added layers of coloured pencil.
