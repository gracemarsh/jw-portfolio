Hi I'm Joyce!

Joyce Wong is a Melbourne based illustrator, specialising in watercolour, gouache and digital painting. She studied Animation and Interactive Media at RMIT University, before completing a Bachelors degree in Health Science, Anatomy and Physiology, at Latrobe University.

Her work largely explores nostalgic notions of childhood, often incorporating floral elements and bright colours. Joyce has opened an Etsy stationery store: Jovi and Blue, which features original characters as well as the occasional fanart print. In her spare time she enjoys watching anime and listening (in excess) to podcasts about film and science.
